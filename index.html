Benefit Appeal in Live Streaming: The Moderating Role of Influencers’ Beauty and Body Motion Magnitude

ABSTRACT

Shifting from conventional media to newly emerging live streaming, the significance of applying benefit appeal is increasing. This three-step empirical study explores the impact of benefit appeal in the live streaming context, examines the correlation between benefit appeal and sales performance, and tests the causality. Results indicate that benefit appeal increases sales performance, with customer engagement mediating this effect, and beauty and body motion magnitude positively moderating the impact.

Multimedia Data Processing

In this research, we collected structured and unstructured data from live-streaming videos.

Interpretable Machine Learning

Interpretable Machine learning methods were applied to explore the impact of variables on sales amount.

Experimental Stimuli in Study 3

Two experiments were conducted to test the causality effect of benefit appeal, beauty, and body motion magnitude.
